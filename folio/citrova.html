Lilliana Checo
citrova
(2018)

Citrova is a juice brand for a healthier lifestyle. Each beverage is crafted with a base of a fruit with antioxidants which is then mix with a citrus fruit and, to lower sugar levels, a dash of spice! Citrova provides you the best combination of nutrients in its stylish and recyclable packaging. Citrova is a group collaboration with fellow designs students at Parsons (Sana Karnawat, Nidhi Raipancholia and Ariana Ushiki) for a Product and Packaging course.

lilliana is currently majoring in communication design and minoring in comics and graphic narrative at parsons school of design.
