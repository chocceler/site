Lilliana Checo
emotion rank
(2018)

The media has a direct impact on our perception of the world around us and, sometimes, it can affect our mood be it good or bad news. emotion rank is a system I created in addition to the 1-10 rating MAL provides its users. Sometimes when you watch a TV show or any content in particular, you might find yourself overwhelmed with emotion yet lacking an explanation for it. With this list, I encourage myself to be more aware of how and why I feel a certain way when watching shows. emotion rank is both a tool of self-reflection and content filter.
