Lilliana Checo
imbricated geometries
(2019)

As an exploration of p5.js methods, imbricated geometries is the product and example of the beauty of randomness. From the scale and amount of triangles to their color and opacity, everything is brought to life with the random attribute. The result is an endless variety of geometric compositions.
